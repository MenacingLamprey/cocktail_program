Summer 2020 Cocktail spec sheet

Gravy Old Fashioned

IngredientAmount
Basil Hayden Bourbon 2 oz
Agave 1/2 oz
Ango. Biters 2 dashes

squeeze agave into julep glass, dash bitters and pour bourbon, stir for about 30 seconds (not much for small ice) pour in to glass, add ice. peel lemon and season rim

Mulo Dulce

IngredientAmount
Lime 1/2 oz
Honey Sage Syrup 3/4 oz
Makers Mark Bourbon 1.5 oz
ginger ale top

pour honey sage syrup, lime and bourbon into rocks glass, fill with ice and then ginger ale. Express sage leaf and serve with lime and stir straws

Sangria

IngredientAmount
sangria 1/2 glass
fruit 1 scoop
soda water top

pour sangria into wine glass, scoop fruit then add ice and top w/ soda water. serve with stir straws

Sutlers Lavender Collins

IngredientAmount
lavender syrup 1/2 oz
lemon juice 1/2 oz
sutlers gin 1.5 oz
soda water top

pour lavender syrup, lemon and gin in to collins glass, fill with ice and soda water. garnish with lemon wheel and lav. buds. Serve with straw

Cucumber Mule

IngredientAmount
Effen Vodka 1.5 oz
lime juice .5 oz
blenhiems gingerale top

pour vodka and lime juice into mug, fill with ice and blenhiems. Add lime and cucumber garnish, serve w/ stir straw.

Aperol Spritz

IngredientAmount
Aperol 1.5 oz
proseco 2 oz
soda water top

pour aperol in to glass, pour proseco, fill w/ ice and soda water. peel orange and season rim. serve w/ stir straw.

Raspberry Lemoncello Martini

IngredientAmount
limencello 1.5 oz
raspberry schnapps 1.5 oz
lemon .75 oz
soda water splash

pour schnapps, lemon and limencello into shaker and shake until tin is lightly frosted. strain in to glass, add splash of soda amd garnish with lemon wheel

Practice What You Peach

IngredientAmount
makers mark bourbon 1.5 oz
Peach Schnapps .5oz
Peach puree .5 oz
Triple Citrus-aide 2 oz

mix all ingredients in tin, add ice and shake. fine strain ingredients in to rocks glass, add ice and garnish.

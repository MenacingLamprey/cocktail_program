Skeleton Key

IngredientVolume
Skeleteon Key Spirit 1.5 oz
Soda Water 4 oz
Green Bitters 4 drops

In collins glass, pour Skeleteon Key Spirit, fill with ice and top with soda water and green bitters. Garnish with skeletonized leaf and serve with straw
